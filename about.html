About Us

We are a group of entrepreneurial students from the Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP) with a passion for good food, great coffee, and affordable prices. We serve delicious, budget-friendly pastries, coffee, doughnuts, and drinks that fit perfectly into every student’s day.

Born from the idea of creating a cozy and accessible space for students to relax, recharge, and refuel, we designed our menu with quality, comfort, and value in mind. Whether you’re pulling an all-nighter, grabbing a quick bite between classes, or just hanging out with friends, we’ve got something for you.

Our Goal

Provide students with affordable, high-quality pastries, coffee, and drinks that support their everyday lifestyle.
Establish a warm and welcoming space that fosters relaxation, productivity, and meaningful connections.
Uphold the spirit of student entrepreneurship by promoting creativity, teamwork, and innovation in our operations.
